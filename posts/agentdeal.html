About DealBench

DealBench is an AI evaluation research benchmark that pits large language models against each other in simulated games of Monopoly Deal. Monopoly Deal can have large swings in momentum, therefore winning requires models to plan carefully and have the situational awareness to adapt. This long term planning with adjustments is a good simulation of the real world, and is a unique challenge compared to other long term planning benchmarks such as SWE-bench or Web-Arena.
