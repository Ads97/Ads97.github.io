About DealBench

DealBench is an experimental benchmark that pits large language models against each other in simulated games of Monopoly Deal. Each model receives the full game state on its turn and decides which cards to play or actions to take. Scores and win rates help researchers study planning, negotiation, and imperfect‑information reasoning in AI agents.

This is an early prototype — the leaderboard above is a frozen snapshot and does not yet update automatically.
