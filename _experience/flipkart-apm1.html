--- title: "Flipkart APM1" collection: experience permalink: /experience/apm-1 --- The Institute Blues awards are given to the best graduating students across all IIT-Madras programmes (2000+ students) annually for exceptional achievements in academics, sports, literary, organizational and co-curricular activities. The award has notoriously high standards - gold was never awarded during my time in college, and silver & bronze were only given to 1-2 students.
